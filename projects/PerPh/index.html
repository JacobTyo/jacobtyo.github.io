Performance Photo - AI for event photography

Performance Photo is an AI powered photography marketplace, where anyone has access to powerful artificial intelligence that can automatically organize event photos by participant. Anyone can add and sell photos. We have multiple papers outlining our technological advancements making this possible under review. Let me know if you'd like too see a preprint of them. At a high level, our technology does not rely on biometrics, can "see through mud" in both re-identification and text spotting contexts, and we introduce a new, highly efficient, labeling methodology. The website is currently shutdown as we begin the process of selling our technology.
